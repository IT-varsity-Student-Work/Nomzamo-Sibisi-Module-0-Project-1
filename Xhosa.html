Eastern Cape

Xhosa Land

The Eastern Cape is a province of South Africa. Its Capital is Bisho,but its two largest cities are Port Elizabeth and East London.It was formed in 1994 out of Xhosa homelands or bantustans of transkei and ciskei, together with the easter portion of the Cape Province.
